methods, user interface ideas and connectivity while keeping

Development Method

Well, it's - letting things evolve. There's a process of tweaking

Programming Philosophy

Upto this day it has been - "don't use anything". Following that

Research!

How Everything Started

The first draft for the site was made around the last two weeks

of September, 2012. It was basically about a network of

people with two location identities - a virtual and a physical one.

The basic idea was to allow people to have random and quick

meetups probably according to their interests.

Since Then?

Things went pretty much all over the social networking

and blogging world since then. The initial goal of project became

'just another feature' after a while. Yet, that 'awhile' was needed

to figure out the basic design of the site.
